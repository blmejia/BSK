Why Community and Brotherhood

We are beings of community, we thrive in numbers. It is crucial for the Afro and or Latino young man to be able to reside in a place he is loved and celebrated. Beta Sigma Kappa Afro-Latino Fraternity Inc, achieves this through Brotherhood



Positon in America, and the World

Current times have shown the world is no cake walk for those of Afro and or Latino/indigenous roots. The fight for equality and overall a firm position in America and the world is needed, the Afro and or Latino young man must know he has Strength and can make a difference.



Preserving Afro-Latino roots

One of our goals is to is to keep the Afro and or Latino young man culturally intact. Our Ancestry is linked to historical greatness and achievement; when graced with knowledge, the young man will better understand himself and the world as it relates to him.



Pipeline to Greatness

Another one of our goals is the "Pipeline to Greatness", with over 60 Brothers of all disciplines; we ensure that a powerful network of men of wisdom, education and courage are here to help mold the future generations of Scorpions.
